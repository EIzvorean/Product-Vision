About Product Vision

What is product vision?

A product vision represents the core essence of its product or product line. It also sets the direction for where a product is headed, or the end state for what a product will deliver in the future. Your vision serves as the north star for every action taken to enhance the product; if adding a feature will not help your product achieve its vision, then the value of that feature should be diminished. This is crucial information that your team must understand to develop and maintain a winning product. To keep your vision grounded in reality, imagine yourself reading reviews about your product in five years. What would you expect those reviews to say?

Edward Izvorean - Thoughts - Understanding product vision is important because if the whole group does not know what they are doing, the overall goal can get lost. We are working towards an end product and if people keep getting side tracked from it, it takes longer to get done, things get missed and mistakes are made.

Matt Carrillo - Thoughts - I think that understanding product vision is important because when a team is analyzing and building each individual piece of a product, it can become easy to lose sight of what the product is supposed to be as a whole.

Justin Baker - Thoughts - Product vision is important in order to understand what you want your final product to be. It allows someone to decide what features it has and the flexibility of the project.
